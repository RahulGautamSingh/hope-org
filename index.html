About Us

Our vision: healthier communities through schools and outreach.

Founded in 2004 by Dr. Gourdas Choudhuri, HOPE uses the "School to Community" philosophy — reaching families through the schools their children attend. From Uttar Pradesh's districts to national conferences, we create lasting change in health awareness, disease prevention, and community wellbeing.

We run focused programs across education, health, and livelihoods to create sustainable impact in the communities we serve.
