Program 01

📚 Education & Literacy

We run 32 learning centres across 5 states, providing free education to over 4,000 children annually. Our curriculum includes foundational literacy, digital skills, and life skills training. Each centre is staffed by trained local educators who understand the community's needs.

Our scholarship program has helped 850+ girls continue their education beyond primary school, with a 92% retention rate.

Program 02

🏥 Healthcare & Nutrition

Our mobile health camps bring primary healthcare to remote villages that have no access to clinics. We conduct monthly camps covering general health check-ups, maternal care, immunisation drives, and nutrition counselling.

In the past year alone, we've conducted 180+ camps serving over 8,000 patients, with a special focus on women's health and childhood malnutrition.

Program 03

🌾 Livelihoods & Skill Training

Economic independence is at the heart of lasting change. Our livelihood programs include tailoring and handicraft workshops, micro-enterprise incubation, and self-help group formation for rural women.

We've helped form 60+ self-help groups across Jharkhand and Bihar, collectively managing over ₹45 lakhs in community savings.

Program 04

💧 Clean Water & Sanitation

Access to clean water changes everything — from health outcomes to school attendance. We install borewells, water purification systems, and conduct sanitation awareness campaigns in partnership with local panchayats.

To date, we've installed 120+ water points serving 35,000+ people, reducing waterborne diseases by an estimated 60% in target areas.
