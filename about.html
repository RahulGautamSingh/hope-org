Our Story

Founded in compassion, driven by purpose.

Hope Foundation began in 2010 with a small group of volunteers determined to change the trajectory of underserved communities across India. What started as weekend teaching sessions under a banyan tree has grown into a multi-program organisation reaching thousands.

We believe in working alongside communities — not for them, but with them — to build solutions that last beyond our presence.

Our Mission

Empower, educate, and elevate.

Our mission is to create inclusive opportunities for children, women, and marginalised groups through education, healthcare, and sustainable livelihood programs. We envision a world where every person has the tools and support to build a dignified life.
