Our Story

Founded in compassion, driven by purpose.

HOPE is a Public Charitable Trust registered under the Bombay Trust Act, dedicated to social, health, and community welfare activities in Navi Mumbai. Our Board comprises professionals, government servants, and business leaders of high repute, united by a shared commitment to improving the lives of those around them.

Based at Nerul Gymkhana in Sector 28, Nerul, we have spent over 15 years working alongside communities — not for them, but with them — building solutions that last well beyond our presence.

Our Vision & Mission

Healthier communities, brighter futures.

Our vision is to improve the health of students, teachers, their families, and other members of the community through schools and community-based outreach. We run programs in preventive health, disease control, youth welfare, and social inclusion — ensuring every person in our community has access to the care and support they deserve.
